How It Works

Whether you're buying through our auction or selling directly — the process is straightforward.

Buying a Vehicle

1

Browse Inventory

Search our full inventory online or visit our lot. Filter by make, model, price, or mileage.

2

Schedule a Test Drive

Book a test drive at your convenience. Our team will have the vehicle ready and waiting for you.

3

Bid or Buy Direct

Place a bid at our live auction or purchase directly from Let's Drive Auto at a fixed price.

4

Drive It Home

Complete paperwork with our finance team and drive away in your new vehicle — same day.

Selling Your Vehicle

1

Submit Your Vehicle

Fill out our simple online form with your vehicle details, photos, and asking price.

2

Get an Appraisal

Our experts will evaluate your vehicle and provide a competitive market valuation within 24 hours.

3

List & Auction

We photograph your vehicle, create a detailed listing, and schedule it for our next auction.

4

Get Paid Fast

Once sold, receive payment within 1–3 business days. We handle all the paperwork.
