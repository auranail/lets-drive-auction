Frequently Asked Questions

▶ How do I register to bid at auction?

Registration is free. Visit us in person with a valid ID and a refundable $500 deposit, or call our team to pre-register before auction day.

▶ Do you offer financing?

Yes! Let's Drive Auto offers competitive financing through our network of lenders. Most approvals happen within 1 hour. Call or visit us for details.

▶ How long does the selling process take?

Most vehicles sell within 1–2 auction cycles (1–2 weeks). Payment is released 1–3 business days after the sale is finalized.
