Our Story

More Than a Dealership

Since 2015, we've been redefining how people buy and sell vehicles in our community — with transparency, technology, and trust.

🏢
Who We Are

Built on Integrity. Driven by Passion.

Let's Drive was founded in 2015 with a simple belief: buying and selling a car shouldn't be complicated, stressful, or opaque. Our founder, a lifelong car enthusiast and former finance professional, saw firsthand how the traditional dealership model failed everyday consumers — hidden fees, lowball trade-in offers, and pressure tactics had become the norm.

We set out to change that. Starting from a single lot with just 20 vehicles, we built a platform rooted in radical transparency, fair pricing, and genuine customer service. Today, Let's Drive operates two interconnected brands — Let's Drive Auction and Let's Drive Auto — serving thousands of buyers and sellers every year.

We're proud of where we came from, but we're even more excited about where we're going. Our investments in technology, inspection quality, and staff training mean every customer experience keeps getting better.

Our Brands

Two Brands, One Mission

Whether you're looking for an auction deal or a streamlined dealership purchase, we have the right platform for you.

🔨

Let's Drive Auction

Our flagship auction platform brings buyers and sellers together through competitive live bidding events. Held weekly, our auctions feature a wide range of vehicles — from everyday commuters to exotic sports cars — all pre-inspected and transparently listed. Sellers typically achieve $2,000–$5,000 above private-sale prices through our competitive bidding environment.

🏪

Let's Drive Auto

Our retail dealership arm offers a traditional buying experience — no bidding required. Browse our certified pre-owned inventory, take a test drive, and purchase at a fixed, fair price. Let's Drive Auto also offers financing assistance, trade-in appraisals, and a full service center for post-purchase support. Every vehicle comes with a limited warranty.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do — from how we inspect vehicles to how we answer the phone — reflects these principles.

🔍

Transparency

No hidden fees, no shady fine print. Every vehicle listing includes its complete history, inspection report, and clear pricing. We believe informed buyers make better decisions — and happier customers.

🤝

Integrity

We do the right thing even when no one is watching. Our team is trained to act as advisors, not salespeople — we'll tell you honestly if a vehicle is or isn't right for you.

🌱

Community

We're proud members of the community we serve. We sponsor local events, partner with charities, and create meaningful employment opportunities for our neighbors.

⚡

Innovation

We continuously invest in technology to improve your experience — better search tools, faster appraisals, and streamlined paperwork so you spend less time at the desk.
